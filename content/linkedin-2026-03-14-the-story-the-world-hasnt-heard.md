## "The Story The World Hasn't Heard"

---

The future doesn't belong to the most intelligent.

It belongs to the most human.

---

Not despite all of that -- because of it.

It's your ability to be fully, unapologetically, deeply human.

To know why you're here.
To know who you are.
To know where you're going.
To know what you must do.

I've spent my life helping people answer those questions -- one conversation, one team, one breath at a time.

That's what I carry inside me.

That's what PVRPOSE AI is built on.

That's the story I'm here to tell. 🌍
