## Key Findings (Product Naming)

**Issues with Command | Amplify | Orchestra:**
1. **PEA problem** -- PVRPOSE + EA shortens to "PEA" in speech. Vegetable association. Dealbreaker for $400/hr buyer verbal credibility.
2. **Mixed metaphors** -- military (Command) + audio (Amplify) + music (Orchestra). No coherent hierarchy.
3. **Orchestra is saturated** -- IBM watsonx Orchestrate, Fabric Orchestra, generic "AI orchestration" is an $11.47B category.
4. **Command has military/authoritarian connotation** -- risky for Canadian collaborative business culture.
5. **Verb inconsistency** -- Command (verb), Amplify (verb), Orchestra (noun). Juan caught this himself.
6. **Every winner in professional services B2B uses functional tier names** -- Clio, Xero, HubSpot, Salesforce. Zero use metaphorical names.
7. **PVRPOSE trademark overlap** -- pvrpose.ai exists as an AI coaching platform in Canada. Worth investigating.

**What works:**
- "Amplify" is the strongest word -- no major conflicts, clear meaning, positive connotation, works in French
- "EA" as category ownership is solid strategy
- Two-door framework (solo vs. with EA) is the right structure
